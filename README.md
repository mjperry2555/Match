Match - Kids Learning Game
A fun, colorful drag-and-match educational app built with Pythonista for iOS.
Features
•  Match uppercase, lowercase letters, and numbers
•  Shape matching (circle, square, triangle, star, cross)
•  Word spelling mode with customizable word list
•  Colorful drag-and-drop gameplay with snap-to-target
•  Voice feedback and sound effects
How to Play
1.  Choose a mode: Capital, Lower case, Numbers, Shapes, or Words
2.  Drag colored pieces to matching targets
3.  Complete each group to advance
Perfect for toddlers and preschoolers learning letters, shapes, and spelling.
